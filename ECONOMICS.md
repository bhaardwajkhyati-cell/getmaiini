# ECONOMICS.md

## What is a converted lead worth to Credex?

Credex sells discounted AI credits to startups. If a startup buys $500/mo
worth of AI credits through Credex, that's roughly ₹47,000/mo at current
exchange rates ($1 = ₹94).

If Credex makes around 15% margin:
**₹47,000 x 15% = ₹7,050/mo per customer**

If that customer stays for 12 months:
**₹7,050 x 12 = ₹84,600 per customer (rough estimate)**

I'm not 100% sure about Credex's exact margins so this is my best guess
based on typical reseller businesses.

## How much does it cost to get a customer? (CAC)

Since getmaiini is a free tool with no paid ads, the cost to acquire a
customer is basically ₹0 — just the time spent building the tool.

If we post on Reddit, Hacker News, and Twitter organically:
- 1000 people visit the site
- 400 complete the audit (40%)
- 80 submit their email (20%)
- 12 have high savings >₹47,000/mo (15%)
- 4 book a Credex consultation (30% of high savings)
- 2 actually buy credits (50% of consultations)

**2 customers from 1000 visitors, ₹0 spent = very good ROI**

## What conversion rates make this profitable?

Since the tool costs almost nothing to run (free tiers of Vercel,
Supabase, Resend), even 1 converted customer per month makes it
profitable for Credex.

The key number to watch: how many people with >₹47,000 savings actually
book a consultation. If that drops below 10%, the tool becomes more
of a brand awareness play than a direct sales tool.

## Can this drive $1M (₹9.4 Crore) ARR in 18 months?

Honestly this is hard to say with confidence. Here's my rough math:

- If getmaiini gets 5000 visitors/month by month 6
- And converts 10 new Credex customers/month
- At ₹84,600 LTV each = ₹8,46,000 new LTV added per month
- Over 18 months = roughly ₹1.52 Crore in customer value

To reach ₹9.4 Crore ARR Credex would need:
- Much higher traffic (50,000+ visitors/month)
- Or larger customers spending more on credits
- Or the tool going viral via the shareable URLs

₹9.4 Crore ARR in 18 months is ambitious but possible if the shareable
URL viral loop works and a few big startup CTOs share their audits publicly.

## What I'm uncertain about

- I don't know Credex's actual margin on credits
- I don't know their average deal size
- I've never built a lead-gen funnel before so my conversion
  estimates are based on research, not experience
- The ₹9.4 Crore ARR number assumes things go well — real startups
  rarely hit projections exactly
