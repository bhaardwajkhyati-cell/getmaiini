## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes, completely free. No credit card, no signup required.
You get your full audit instantly.

**Q: What AI tools do you audit?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Anthropic API,
OpenAI API, and Windsurf. More tools coming soon.

**Q: How accurate are the savings estimates?**
A: All pricing data is pulled from official vendor pricing pages
and verified weekly. The savings calculations are based on your
actual plan and seat count — not estimates.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: We store your audit results (tools and savings numbers) to
generate your shareable link. We never store payment information.
Email is only collected if you choose to get your report emailed.

**Q: Who is this for?**
A: Anyone paying for AI tools — from solo developers to engineering
teams. Most useful for teams with 3+ AI tools or 3+ seats on any plan.
